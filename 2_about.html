About Me

I'm Prathamesh, a B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering (AI) student at KLE Technological University, Hubballi. I'm passionate about building full-stack applications that solve real financial and productivity problems for everyday users.

I designed and developed Fingroww — a comprehensive personal finance dashboard for Indian professionals. The project gave me deep hands-on experience with Next.js 14 App Router, Supabase (PostgreSQL with Row Level Security), AI integration via Groq + Llama 3.3-70B, financial calculator design, and writing 647 automated tests across 28 test suites.

Beyond coding, I'm interested in system design, AI applications, and building developer tools. I believe clean architecture matters more than clever code.
